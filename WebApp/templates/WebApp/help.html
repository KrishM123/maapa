File Upload

To upload a file, follow these steps:
 1. Go to "Add Resource" under "Resources"
 2. Click the "Choose File" button
 3. Choose the .pdf to upload

Asking Questions

To ask a question, follow these steps:
 1. Go to "Query"
 2. Choose the file you would like to ask the question
  from

After choosing the file:
 1. Enter question in the box
 2. Click Submit

After clicking submit, the bot will highlight a paragraph from the textbook which probably contains the answer

Upon reading the paragraph, indicate whether the chosen paragraph is correct or not

If the chosen paragraph is not correct, the bot will highlight another paragraph

After indicating that the chosen page was correct, you will be provided with an answer
